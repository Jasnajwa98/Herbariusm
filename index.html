Herbarium

What is a herbarium?

Herbarium can be defined into two terms:

  1. A dried plant collection and arranged according to its appropriate labels.
  2. A building to store these collections.

In brief, Herbarium is basically a library/museum for preserved plants. Inside of the Herbarium, the specimens are neatly arranged for the usage of researchers, students, and even historical researches. The specimens are either pressed and stored in cabinets, preserved in liquid in jars, or air-dried and kept inboxes. Well-kept specimens can last for hundreds of years.
